How It Works

The exam approach.

Every lab mirrors the Red Hat exam workflow. No walkthroughs — treat each question exactly like the real thing.

01
Read the Scenario

Understand context, constraints, and objective before touching your cluster.

02
Plan Your Approach

Decide the command sequence. Think like an administrator, not a student.

03
Execute on Cluster

Use oc CLI or Web Console — your environment, your execution.

04
Verify the Outcome

Confirm objectives are fully — not partially — met before moving on.

05
Fix & Move On

Correct mistakes before proceeding. Partial marks cost you on the real exam.

Exam Mindset

Think like an administrator.

🎯
Accuracy Over Speed

Every command should be deliberate. Avoid experimenting on exam day — a wrong config is worse than a slow one.

✅
Verify Every Step

Use oc get, oc describe, and oc auth can-i to confirm each stage. Verification is your safety net.

⚡
Imperative Over YAML

Use oc adm and oc create flags for speed. Only open YAML editors when strictly required by the task.

🏃
Partial Work Costs Marks

Fix issues before moving on. 80% complete still loses full marks. Clean up and confirm before you proceed.
